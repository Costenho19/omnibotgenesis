## 1. PURPOSE

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship, partnership, technical collaboration, or integration involving OMNIX Decision Governance Infrastructure and Skilligen HDI's Human Decision Infrastructure platform (the "Purpose"). In connection with this Purpose, each Party may disclose to the other certain confidential and proprietary information.

---

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either Party to the other, either directly or indirectly, in writing, orally, electronically, or by inspection of tangible objects, that is designated as "confidential" or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure.

Confidential Information includes, without limitation:

- Proprietary algorithms, scoring logic, checkpoint architectures (including the OMNIX 11-checkpoint governance pipeline), and decision pipeline design
- Post-quantum cryptographic implementations (CRYSTALS-Dilithium3/5, ML-DSA-65/87), key management schemes, and security architecture
- Internal thresholds, weights, and governance parameters
- Source code, technical documentation, and Architectural Decision Records (ADRs)
- Business plans, financial projections, investor materials, and commercial terms
- Customer lists, pipeline, and partnership terms
- Skilligen HDI's HDI platform architecture, methodologies, and client data frameworks
- Any information marked "CONFIDENTIAL", "PROPRIETARY", or equivalent

---

## 3. OBLIGATIONS

Each Receiving Party agrees to:

a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence using at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care

b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party

c) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose described in Section 1

d) Limit disclosure to employees, contractors, or advisors who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations no less restrictive than those in this Agreement

e) Immediately notify the Disclosing Party upon discovery of any unauthorized use or disclosure of Confidential Information

f) Not use the Confidential Information to develop competing products or services

---

## 4. EXCLUSIONS

Obligations under this Agreement do not apply to information that:

a) Is or becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement by the Receiving Party

b) Was rightfully known to the Receiving Party before disclosure, without restriction

c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information

d) Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order — provided the Receiving Party gives the Disclosing Party prompt written notice and reasonable assistance to seek a protective order

---

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Nothing in this Agreement grants either Party any license, right, or interest in the other Party's intellectual property, patents, trademarks, copyrights, or trade secrets. Disclosure of Confidential Information does not constitute a transfer of ownership.

All proprietary technology, methodologies, and intellectual property of OMNIX — including the 11-checkpoint governance pipeline, post-quantum receipt architecture, ADRs, and scoring logic — remains the exclusive property of OMNIX Quantum Ltd and Harold Alberto Nunes Rodelo.

All proprietary technology, methodologies, and intellectual property of Skilligen HDI remains the exclusive property of Skilligen HDI and Dr. Amanulla Khan.

---

## 6. NO REVERSE ENGINEERING

Each Party agrees not to reverse engineer, decompile, disassemble, or attempt to derive the source code, architecture, algorithms, or underlying logic of any software, system, or technology disclosed by the other Party under this Agreement.

---

## 7. NON-SOLICITATION

During the term of this Agreement and for a period of **two (2) years** thereafter, neither Party shall directly solicit for employment any employee, contractor, or advisor of the other Party who was involved in activities related to this Agreement, without prior written consent.

---

## 8. TERM

This Agreement shall remain in effect for **three (3) years** from the Effective Date (i.e., until 14 April 2029). Obligations with respect to trade secrets shall survive indefinitely.

---

## 9. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION OF INFORMATION

Upon written request by either Party, or upon termination of this Agreement, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or certify destruction of all Confidential Information, including all copies, notes, and derivatives thereof, within 10 business days.

---

## 10. REMEDIES

The Parties acknowledge that breach of this Agreement would cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be an inadequate remedy. Each Party agrees that the other Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief, specific performance, or other equitable remedies in addition to all other remedies available at law, without the need to post bond.

---
