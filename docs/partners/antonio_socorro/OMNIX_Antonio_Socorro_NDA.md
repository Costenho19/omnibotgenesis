─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Purpose
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The parties wish to explore a structured technical collaboration
involving an architectural crosswalk between CAI-EXPERT-LAB and
the OMNIX Agent Trust Fabric (ATF), including comparative analysis
of governance invariants, runtime admissibility models, semantic
drift protocols, and cross-framework interoperability.

In connection with this collaboration, each party may disclose
confidential information to the other. This Agreement governs the
terms under which such information is shared, used, and protected.


─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2. Definition of Confidential Information
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Confidential Information means any technical, architectural,
commercial, or operational information disclosed by one party to
the other in connection with the collaboration, including but
not limited to:

- Protocol specifications, field definitions, invariant sets,
  and trace formats
- API architecture, endpoint specifications, and governance logic
- Authority chain design, cryptographic methods, and receipt
  structures
- Runtime governance models, continuity mechanisms, and
  admissibility logic
- Governance methodology, posture-state transition models,
  execution gating architectures, and semantic drift frameworks
- Business strategy, commercial terms, and partner relationships
- Any information marked as confidential or that a reasonable
  party would understand to be confidential given the context
  of disclosure

For the avoidance of doubt: the OMNIX ATF invariants
(ATF-INV-001 through ATF-INV-006 and all extended invariant
sets), the Delegation Receipt (DR), Temporal Admissibility
Record (TAR), Runtime Continuity Record (RCR) structures, and
all associated governance logic are the sole intellectual
property of OMNIX QUANTUM LTD and constitute Confidential
Information of Party B.


─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3. Obligations
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Each party agrees to:

- Hold the other party's Confidential Information in strict
  confidence
- Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of the
  collaboration described in Section 1
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party
  without prior written consent
- Limit access to Confidential Information to personnel who
  have a need to know for the collaboration
- Apply at minimum the same degree of care used to protect its
  own confidential information, and no less than reasonable care
- Not incorporate, adapt, or derive work products from the
  other party's Confidential Information outside the scope of
  this collaboration without explicit written authorisation


─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4. No Reverse Engineering — No Derivative Governance Frameworks
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The receiving party agrees not to:

- Reverse engineer, decompile, reconstruct, or attempt to derive
  the architectural logic, invariant structure, or governance
  methodology of the disclosing party from any disclosed
  Confidential Information
- Develop, publish, or distribute any framework, protocol,
  standard, or product that is derived from, substantially
  replicates, or constitutes a conceptual reproduction of the
  disclosing party's governance architecture, admissibility
  models, or runtime continuity mechanisms
- Use Confidential Information to construct derivative governance
  frameworks or to validate, benchmark, or certify third-party
  systems without the prior written consent of the disclosing
  party

This clause survives termination of this Agreement.


─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5. No Transfer of Governance Methodology Rights
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Nothing in this Agreement transfers any intellectual property
rights, governance methodology rights, or operational framework
rights between the parties. Each party retains full and exclusive
ownership of its existing intellectual property.

For the avoidance of doubt: no licence, ownership transfer, or
implied rights to reproduce OMNIX governance methodologies,
admissibility architectures, runtime continuity models, semantic
drift protocols, or ATF invariant structures are granted to
CAI-EXPERT-LAB under this Agreement — whether express, implied,
by estoppel, or otherwise.

Disclosure of Confidential Information does not grant any
licence, right, or interest in the disclosing party's
intellectual property.

Any joint output produced under this collaboration (including
crosswalk documents, comparative analyses, interoperability
matrices, or joint publications) requires prior written approval
from both parties before publication or external distribution.


─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6. Exclusions
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Obligations under this Agreement do not apply to information that:

- Is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this
  Agreement (including information published under DOI by either
  party prior to this Agreement)
- Was rightfully known to the receiving party prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed by the receiving party without use
  of Confidential Information
- Is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided
  the disclosing party is given reasonable prior written notice


─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
7. Term and Termination
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This Agreement is effective from the Effective Date above and
continues for two (2) years. Either party may terminate this
Agreement with 30 days written notice.

Confidentiality obligations, the no reverse engineering clause
(Section 4), and the no transfer of methodology rights clause
(Section 5) survive termination for a period of three (3) years.


─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
8. Governing Law
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance
with applicable law. Both parties agree to resolve disputes
through good faith negotiation before pursuing any formal
legal process.


─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
9. Entire Agreement
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the
parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes
all prior discussions and agreements relating to confidentiality
between the parties. Any amendment must be made in writing and
signed by both parties.
